Privacy Policy for FaithFlow

Last updated: June 30, 2026

FaithFlow is designed to be private by default. The app works on your device, does not require an account, and does not collect personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information.

FaithFlow does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, tracking tools, user accounts, or our own servers. We do not collect, sell, rent, or share your personal data.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

FaithFlow may store app preferences locally on your device, such as:

  • language preference;
  • selected goals or moods;
  • reminder settings;
  • saved verses;
  • widget theme preferences;
  • reflection notes you choose to write;
  • subscription status provided through Apple StoreKit.

This information stays on your device. We do not receive or access it.

If your device backup settings are enabled, iOS or Apple services may include app data in your device backup according to Apple's own policies.

3. Notifications

If you enable prayer reminders, FaithFlow schedules local notifications on your device.

We do not operate a push notification server, and reminder content is not sent to us.

4. Photos

If you choose to save a verse share card to Photos, FaithFlow may request permission to add the generated image to your photo library.

FaithFlow does not read, upload, scan, or collect your photos.

5. Purchases and Subscriptions

FaithFlow Plus subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store.

We do not receive your payment card details or billing information. Subscription management, renewals, refunds, and payment processing are handled by Apple.

You can manage or cancel subscriptions in your Apple Account settings.

6. Network, Analytics, and Tracking

FaithFlow does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking.

The app's core content is included in the app and can work without our servers. Some actions, such as opening Apple subscription management, App Store purchase flows, privacy policy links, or terms links, may use Apple or browser services.

7. Children's Privacy

FaithFlow does not knowingly collect data from children or from any user. Because we do not collect personal data, we do not knowingly store personal information from children.

8. Data Deletion

Because FaithFlow stores app data locally on your device, you can delete local app data by deleting the app from your device.

Subscription records are managed by Apple and can be managed through your Apple Account.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

adam936@163.com

FaithFlow 隐私政策

最后更新：2026 年 6 月 30 日

FaithFlow 默认以隐私为核心设计。本 App 在你的设备上运行，不需要账号，也不收集个人数据。

1. 我们收集的信息

我们不收集个人信息。

FaithFlow 不使用分析 SDK、广告 SDK、追踪工具、用户账号系统或我们自己的服务器。我们不会收集、出售、出租或分享你的个人数据。

2. 存储在你设备上的信息

FaithFlow 可能会在你的设备本地保存一些 App 偏好设置，例如：

  • 语言偏好；
  • 你选择的目标或情绪；
  • 提醒时间设置；
  • 收藏的经文；
  • 小组件主题偏好；
  • 你主动写下的反思笔记；
  • 由 Apple StoreKit 提供的订阅状态。

这些信息保留在你的设备上。我们不会接收或访问这些信息。

如果你开启了设备备份，iOS 或 Apple 服务可能会按照 Apple 自身政策将 App 数据包含在设备备份中。

3. 通知

如果你开启祷告提醒，FaithFlow 会在你的设备上安排本地通知。

我们不运营推送通知服务器，提醒内容也不会发送给我们。

4. 照片

如果你选择将经文分享卡片保存到照片，FaithFlow 可能会请求权限，将你生成的图片添加到照片图库。

FaithFlow 不会读取、上传、扫描或收集你的照片。

5. 购买与订阅

FaithFlow Plus 订阅由 Apple 通过 App Store 处理。

我们不会接收你的银行卡信息或账单信息。订阅管理、续订、退款和付款处理均由 Apple 负责。

你可以在 Apple 账户设置中管理或取消订阅。

6. 网络、分析与追踪

FaithFlow 不使用第三方分析、广告或追踪服务。

App 的核心内容随 App 一起提供，不依赖我们的服务器。某些操作，例如打开 Apple 订阅管理、App Store 购买流程、隐私政策链接或服务条款链接，可能会使用 Apple 或浏览器服务。

7. 儿童隐私

FaithFlow 不会有意收集儿童或任何用户的数据。由于我们不收集个人数据，因此我们也不会有意保存儿童的个人信息。

8. 数据删除

由于 FaithFlow 将 App 数据保存在你的设备本地，你可以通过从设备中删除 App 来删除本地 App 数据。

订阅记录由 Apple 管理，可通过你的 Apple 账户进行管理。

9. 本政策的变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。如有更新，我们会修改上方的“最后更新”日期。

10. 联系我们
